# Locations in The Abyss

## (0, 0, 0)
- **Name**: The Hole
- **Description**: The starting location where you fell into the Abyss. It's dark, damp, and the only way out seems to be further into the cave.

## (1, 0, 0)
- **Name**: Narrow Passage
- **Description**: A narrow passage leading deeper into the cave. The walls are closing in, and the air feels thicker here.

## (2, 0, 0)
- **Name**: Underground Lake
- **Description**: A hidden lake within the cave, the water is crystal clear but ice-cold. There's a faint glow from bioluminescent algae.

## (1, 1, 0)
- **Name**: Old Mine Shaft
- **Description**: An old, abandoned mine shaft. The wooden beams are rotting, and the ceiling looks like it could collapse at any moment.

## (0, 1, 0)
- **Name**: Collapsed Tunnel
- **Description**: The tunnel has collapsed here. Debris and rocks block the path forward, but there might be another way around.

## (1, 0, 1)
- **Name**: Crystal Cavern
- **Description**: A cavern filled with sparkling crystals. The light reflects off every surface, creating a mesmerizing effect.

## (0, 0, -1)
- **Name**: Hidden Chamber
- **Description**: A hidden chamber discovered by accident. There are strange markings on the walls, possibly left by those who came before.

## (-1, 0, 0)
- **Name**: Cave Entrance
- **Description**: The entrance to the cave system, partially obscured by overgrown vines. The outside world seems far away from here.

## (2, 1, 0)
- **Name**: Echoing Chamber
- **Description**: A large chamber where every sound is amplified. You can hear your footsteps echoing off the distant walls.

## (1, -1, 0)
- **Name**: Abandoned Camp
- **Description**: An old campsite with remnants of a fire pit and broken equipment. It looks like someone left in a hurry.

## (2, 1, 1)
- **Name**: Stalactite Hall
- **Description**: A vast hall filled with enormous stalactites and stalagmites. The formations create natural obstacles and pathways.

## (3, 1, 0)
- **Name**: Mushroom Grove
- **Description**: A grove of giant mushrooms with bioluminescent caps. The light they emit creates an eerie but beautiful environment.

## (2, 2, 0)
- **Name**: Underground River
- **Description**: A fast-flowing river cutting through the cave. The water is too swift to cross without a raft or bridge.

## (0, 2, 0)
- **Name**: Miner’s Hut
- **Description**: A small, dilapidated hut used by miners long ago. Inside, you find old tools and a journal with cryptic notes.

## (3, 0, 0)
- **Name**: Collapsed Bridge
- **Description**: A once-grand bridge that has collapsed into the abyss below. You might be able to find a way across or climb down to the lower levels.

## (-2, 1, 0)
- **Name**: Ancient Ruins
- **Description**: The remains of a forgotten civilization’s structures. Strange symbols and artifacts hint at a mysterious past.

## (0, 2, 1)
- **Name**: Luminous Cavern
- **Description**: A cavern illuminated by glowing fungi and minerals. The light here is soft and magical, creating a serene atmosphere.

## (1, 1, -1)
- **Name**: Cave Waterfall
- **Description**: A stunning waterfall cascading into a pool below. The mist creates a refreshing environment, and the pool might be a clue to hidden passages.

## (2, 0, 2)
- **Name**: Crystal Bridge
- **Description**: A natural bridge made of crystal formations. It’s precarious to cross but offers a great view of the surrounding caverns.

## (1, 2, 1)
- **Name**: Fungal Forest
- **Description**: A dense area of towering fungi and mushrooms. The environment is both exotic and dangerous, with spores that might cause hallucinations.

## (-1, 1, -1)
- **Name**: Depths of Echo
- **Description**: A deep, dark area where your voice echoes endlessly. The darkness here is almost tangible, making navigation challenging.

## (0, -2, 0)
- **Name**: Collapsed Cavern
- **Description**: A large cavern that has partially collapsed, creating a jumble of rocks and debris. A path might be hidden among the rubble.
